# AI Business OS Master Admin

AI Business OS Master Admin is the private control center for building, operating, updating, and supporting the AI Business OS platform.

This repository is the technical source of truth for the Master Admin application, the company knowledge base, architecture decisions, engineering rules, and operating model for the first commercial product: AI Marketing Team.

## What This Company Is Building

AI Business OS is a modular operating system for business work powered by AI employees, reusable engines, provider abstractions, and human approval workflows.

The first revenue product is AI Marketing Team: a customer-facing AI marketing department that helps business owners create content, prepare campaigns, build landing pages, approve output, publish work, analyze results, and improve conversion.

## Repository Role

This repository owns:

- Master Admin documentation and future implementation.
- Architecture decisions and company engineering rules.
- Product direction for AI Business OS and AI Marketing Team.
- Shared concepts used by the future Client Template repository.
- Codex and Lovable working instructions.

The Client Template is a separate cloneable customer application. Master Admin manages templates, packages, updates, prompts, employees, client health, and support access.

## Source of Truth

GitHub is the single source of truth for technical documentation, architecture, project memory, and future code.

If another tool or conversation conflicts with this repository, this repository wins until updated by a documented decision.

## Core Principles

- Revenue first, platform always.
- Sell outcomes, not tools.
- Sell employees, not agents.
- Keep human approval central.
- Keep the Client Template cloneable.
- Build reusable engines and modules.
- Document important decisions before or alongside implementation.
