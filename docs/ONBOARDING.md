# Onboarding

Onboarding turns a closed customer into an active customer with a configured AI Marketing Team.

## Onboarding Goals

- Capture accurate Business DNA.
- Configure the client installation.
- Enable the right package.
- Load useful knowledge.
- Introduce AI employees.
- Produce first approved output.
- Confirm support expectations.

## Onboarding Steps

1. Create or prepare Client Template clone.
2. Configure environment and credentials.
3. Select package.
4. Complete Business DNA.
5. Add initial knowledge records.
6. Enable first employees.
7. Run content workflow.
8. Review and approve first output.
9. Confirm analytics and support channels.
10. Mark customer active.

## Business DNA Intake

Business DNA should be gathered through a guided flow, not a vague blank form.

Required information should include offers, audience, voice, services, restrictions, conversion goals, channels, and examples of good marketing.

## Success Criteria

Onboarding is successful when the customer has approved at least one useful output and understands how to request more work.
