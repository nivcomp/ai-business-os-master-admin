# Employee Catalog

The Employee Catalog defines the AI employees that AI Business OS can sell, install, configure, and manage.

Customers should experience these as employees in their business, not as technical agents.

## Catalog Strategy

Start with a sellable AI Marketing Team, then expand into other office departments after the first package proves revenue, onboarding, support, and retention.

Each employee should be packaged with responsibilities, workflows, prompt packages, tools, provider needs, approval rules, and success metrics.

## First Department: AI Marketing Team

### Marketing Manager

Purpose: plans campaigns, coordinates marketing work, creates briefs, reviews output, and recommends next actions.

Core workflows:

- Create monthly marketing plan.
- Create campaign brief.
- Assign content tasks.
- Review campaign performance.
- Recommend improvements.

Provider needs:

- Text and reasoning.
- Analytics.
- CRM Lite.
- Knowledge Base.

### Copywriter

Purpose: creates marketing copy that matches Business DNA, brand voice, offers, audience, and channel.

Core workflows:

- Social post draft.
- Email draft.
- Landing page copy.
- Ad copy variations.
- CTA improvement.

Provider needs:

- Text generation.
- Knowledge retrieval.
- Optional SEO provider.

### Designer

Purpose: creates and adapts visual assets for campaigns.

Core workflows:

- Social image concept.
- Ad creative draft.
- Thumbnail draft.
- Brand-consistent image variation.

Provider needs:

- Image generation.
- Image editing.
- Asset storage.
- Brand asset access.

### Video Creator

Purpose: creates video scripts, short-form video concepts, storyboards, and eventually generated clips.

Core workflows:

- Short video script.
- Reel concept.
- Product explainer draft.
- Video ad variation.

Provider needs:

- Text generation.
- Video generation.
- Voice/audio provider.
- Asset storage.

### SEO Manager

Purpose: improves search visibility and content structure.

Core workflows:

- Keyword topic plan.
- SEO brief.
- Blog outline.
- Landing page SEO review.
- Metadata suggestions.

Provider needs:

- Text generation.
- Search or SEO data provider.
- Analytics.

### Campaign Manager

Purpose: turns approved strategy and content into scheduled campaigns.

Core workflows:

- Build campaign calendar.
- Prepare channel-specific posts.
- Schedule approved content.
- Track publishing status.

Provider needs:

- Social publishing.
- Email.
- WhatsApp or messaging.
- Calendar or scheduling.

### Analytics Manager

Purpose: turns performance data into simple business recommendations.

Core workflows:

- Summarize campaign performance.
- Explain what worked.
- Identify underperforming content.
- Recommend next experiments.

Provider needs:

- Analytics.
- CRM Lite.
- Text and reasoning.

## Future Departments

After AI Marketing Team, possible employee groups include:

- Sales: Lead Qualifier, Follow-Up Assistant, Proposal Writer, CRM Manager.
- Support: Support Agent, FAQ Manager, Ticket Summarizer.
- Operations: Task Coordinator, Process Writer, Automation Manager.
- Finance: Invoice Assistant, Expense Summarizer, Cashflow Analyst.
- Management: Executive Assistant, KPI Analyst, Strategy Assistant.

## Employee Definition Template

Each employee should define:

- Customer-facing name.
- Department.
- Business promise.
- Responsibilities.
- Inputs required.
- Outputs produced.
- Prompt packages used.
- Tools available.
- Provider dependencies.
- Approval requirements.
- Success metrics.
- Support and escalation rules.

## Selling Rule

Sell employees as outcomes and roles:

- Better marketing execution.
- Faster content production.
- More consistent brand voice.
- Clear approval and control.
- Less operational delay.

Do not sell raw agents, prompts, or APIs to normal customers.
