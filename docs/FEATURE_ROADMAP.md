# Feature Roadmap

The feature roadmap translates the company vision into product capabilities.

## Product Sequence

The first product is AI Marketing Team. Future products may add more departments after the first product proves revenue, onboarding, retention, and support.

## Phase 1 Features

Phase 1 should focus on the smallest sellable AI Marketing Team:

- Business DNA onboarding.
- Knowledge base foundation.
- AI Marketing Manager.
- AI Copywriter.
- Content creation workflow.
- Approval Center.
- Landing page workflow.
- CRM Lite foundation.
- Basic analytics.
- Clone setup checklist.

## Phase 2 Features

Phase 2 expands platform leverage:

- Package system.
- Employee Store.
- More marketing employees.
- Advanced analytics.
- Publishing integrations.
- Automation builder.
- Update Center.
- Client Health Center.

## Phase 3 Features

Phase 3 moves toward the wider AI Business OS:

- Sales department.
- Support department.
- Operations workflows.
- Public API.
- Developer platform.
- Enterprise controls.

## Prioritization Rules

Prioritize features that:

- Help sell the first product.
- Improve repeatable onboarding.
- Strengthen reusable platform foundations.
- Increase customer trust through approval and visibility.
- Reduce future support burden.
