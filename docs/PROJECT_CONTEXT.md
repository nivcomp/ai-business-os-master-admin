# Project Context

AI Business OS is a long-term platform for building, managing, deploying and improving AI-powered business teams.

## Core Direction

We are not building only an app. We are building the foundation of a software company.

The first commercial product is AI Marketing Team: a customer-facing AI team that helps business owners create content, plan campaigns, build landing pages, approve work, publish content, analyze results and improve conversion.

## Two Repository Architecture

- `ai-business-os-master-admin`: the private control center used by Yaniv to manage clients, templates, prompts, employees, versions, updates and support.
- `ai-business-os-client-template`: the cloneable client system used as a base for each client installation.

## Customer Language

Customers should experience the product as a business team:

- AI Business Team
- Employees

- Office

- Approval Center

## Internal Technical Language

## Current Strategy

Revenue first, platform always.

The first product must be sellable fast, but every major component should strengthen the long-term AI Business OS platform.
