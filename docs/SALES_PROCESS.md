# Sales Process

The sales process should sell business progress, not technical AI complexity.

## Target Customer

Initial target customers are small and medium businesses that need marketing execution but do not have enough internal capacity, consistency, or speed.

Good early customers:

- Need frequent content.
- Have clear offers.
- Can approve work quickly.
- Understand the value of marketing consistency.
- Are willing to participate in onboarding.

## Sales Message

Primary message: hire an AI Marketing Team that helps create, review, and improve marketing work.

Avoid leading with agents, prompt engineering, model names, or integrations.

## Sales Stages

1. Identify business pain.
2. Confirm marketing workflow gap.
3. Demonstrate employee/team concept.
4. Show approval-centered output.
5. Explain onboarding and package.
6. Confirm pricing and support expectations.
7. Close and schedule onboarding.

## Demo Requirements

A demo should show:

- Business DNA.
- AI employee output.
- Approval flow.
- Simple analytics or progress signal.
- How the customer remains in control.
