# UX Principles

UX should help customers feel they are managing a useful AI business team.

## Core Experience

The customer should be able to:

- Onboard the business through Business DNA.
- Understand which employees are available.
- Ask for useful marketing work.
- Review and approve output.
- See what happened and what improved.

## Trust Principles

Trust is built through:

- Human approval.
- Clear drafts.
- Editable outputs.
- Visible status.
- Explainable context.
- Consistent brand voice.
- Recoverable mistakes.

## First-Use Experience

The first-use flow should lead the customer toward value quickly:

1. Explain the business.
2. Confirm Business DNA.
3. Meet the first AI employees.
4. Generate a useful draft.
5. Review and approve it.
6. Save or use the result.

## Avoid

- Technical setup before value.
- Overwhelming dashboards.
- Agent terminology in customer UI.
- Automation without trust.
- Hidden provider complexity.
