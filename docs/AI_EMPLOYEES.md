# AI Employees

AI Employees are the customer-facing model for AI workers inside AI Business OS.

Customers hire and manage employees. Internally, an employee may be powered by agents, prompts, tools, knowledge, memory, workflow rules, and provider calls.

## First Employees

The AI Marketing Team should start with:

- Marketing Manager
- Copywriter
- Designer
- SEO Manager
- Campaign Manager
- Analytics Manager

Not every employee needs to be fully automated in the first version. Some may begin as guided workflows with AI assistance.

## Employee Definition

Each employee should define:

- Customer-facing name.
- Department.
- Responsibilities.
- Inputs required.
- Outputs produced.
- Tools available.
- Knowledge access.
- Approval requirements.
- Quality criteria.
- Escalation rules.

## Internal Agent Mapping

An employee may map to one or more internal agents.

Internal agents should stay implementation details unless the surface is built for administrators or engineers.

## Approval Rule

Customer-facing work produced by an AI employee should require human approval when it affects brand, publishing, customer communication, billing, or external systems.
